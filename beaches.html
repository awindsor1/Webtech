Beaches in Stuart

Stuart has a large variety of beaches that will meet everyone's needs! Look below for the best beaches and some information about each.

Stuart Beach

  • The full "beach" experience
  • Lifeguards
  • Concession stand
  • Plenty of parking
  • Large crowds

Bathtub beach

  • Constant low tide
  • Great for little kids
  • The best shells
  • Minimal parking
  • No lifeguard

Tiger Shores Beach

  • No lifeguard
  • Private atmosphere
  • Walking distance to grocery store
  • Parking lot is short distance to beach
  • No crowds

House of Refuge Beach

  • No lifeguards
  • Rocky
  • Good for exploring
  • Big waves
  • Quiet
