Sailfish Splash Waterpark

Established in 2013, Sailfish Splash is home to an olympic size pool, swimming lessons, water slides, playgrounds, and a lazy river. There is something for everyone! Check out the prices below.

House of Refuge

The House of Refuge is the only one left remaining of its kind. Originally one of a dozen throughout the Treasure Coast, this lone survivor is the oldest building in Stuart, Florida. It was used as a refuge for sailor's in trouble in the late 1800s and early 1900s. The House of Refuge is a monumental part of Stuart, and is a must-see for tourists and residents of Stuart!

Elliott Museum

The Elliott Museum is a newly built museum located right next to Stuart Beach. It was donated by the Elliott Family and hosts different historic exhibits every few months. The evolving exhibits allow residents to visit the museum multiple times and see new things every visit!

The Lyric Theater

The Lyric Theater is a recently renovated theater located in the heart of Stuart in the historic downtown area. The theater holds anything from professional dance performances to local comedy acts. With a multitude of fine-dining restaurants right around the corner, a trip to the Lyric Theater is sure to be a great evening!

The Heritage Museum

The Heritage Museum is a museum in the downtown area preserving artifacts from the city of Stuart. What used to be the "Stuart Feed Supply", the only market in Stuart in the early 1900s, is now a museum that shows off the history of Stuart. You can find anything from paintings to shipwrecked objects in this quaint shop.
