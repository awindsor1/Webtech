Allison's Project Proposal

What is my Website About?

My website will be about Stuart, Florida. It is a small, quaint town on the east coast of Florida. As my hometown, I want people to be able to have a better understanding of what this town has to offer.

What is the Objective of my Website?

The objective of my website will be to inform people of an unknown vacation destination. I will explain the history of the town as well as include attractions and things to do in the area.

Who is my Target Audience?

My target audience is either people who want to vacation in Stuart, or people who want to be informed about a great town that little is known about.

What content do I plan to include in each page of my website?

  • Home page: an overview of the location of the town and highlights of what is available to do that will link back to each page
  • History: a page dedicated to the history of the town and its origins
  • Attractions: give details about what is available to do in Stuart and popular activities
  • Dining: the best restaurants and a brief synopsis of the atmosphere
  • Rentals: Links to the best hotels and rentals in the area and an explanation of which is best for various types of travelers
